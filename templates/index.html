Key Features

Unsupervised AI

Convolutional autoencoder reconstructs the MRI and highlights suspicious regions through reconstruction error without class-based supervision.

Heatmap Visualization

Color-coded anomaly heatmaps, overlay views, and binary masks help show where the reconstruction error is highest.

Normal vs Abnormal Output

The system reports whether the uploaded MRI appears Normal or Abnormal and supports that decision with anomaly score and reconstruction loss.

Secure Access Control
